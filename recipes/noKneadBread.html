No Knead Bread

Back To Home

Recipe from Lindsay Ostrom

Description

An easy to make bread recipe with no kneading necessary.
The bread features a crispy outside and a soft chewy inside.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 & 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon instant yeast
  • 1 & 1/2 cups room temperature water
