No Knead Bread

Back To Home

Recipe from Lindsay Ostrom

Description

An easy to make bread recipe with no kneading necessary.
The bread features a crispy outside and a soft chewy inside.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 & 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon instant yeast
  • 1 & 1/2 cups room temperature water

Steps

  1. In a large mixing bowl, whisk the flour, salt, and yeast together until mixed. Stir in the water until a chunky, thick dough forms.
  2. Cover the mixing bowl with plastic wrap and let it rest for 12-18 hours at room temperature.
  3. Preheat your oven to 450 Fahrenheit, and stick a 6 quart enamel coated cast iron Lodge Dutch Oven (or similar) in the oven for about 30 minutes to heat.
  4. Gently scrape the dough out onto a well-floured surface, shape it into a ball with flour on the outside, set on a piece of parchment, and cover with plastic while your Dutch Oven heats up.
  5. Remove the plastic from the dough. Lift the dough and parchment together into the pan so the parchment lines the bottom of the hot pan.
  6. Bake, covered, for 30 minutes.
  7. Remove the cover and bake another 10-15 minutes.
